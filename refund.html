Overview

We value our customers and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchases. Below are the details of our refund policy.

1. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds are available for products that are defective or damaged during shipping. Refund requests must be made within 30 days of receiving your order.

2. Refund Process

Once a refund request is approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original payment method. It may take several business days to reflect in your account.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Certain items, such as gift cards, are non-refundable.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding refunds, please contact us at support@bookcave.com.
